What's Foodictive ?

Foodictive is an app intended to help tourist to identify food that they have. Foodictive will identify what kind of food is that and show detailed information about that food.

How to Use Foodictive

Our app can be used easily. All you need is just a food and Foodictive app. Don't have any food yet? You can also use a photo of food in your phone. Our developer also provide a printed food card that can be your starterpack. You can get this card by clicking on this link. Here are some step-by-step tutorial to guide you using our app.

  • Install Foodictive on your phone.
  • Open the app and allow the permissions.
  • Swipe to the right side of your phone to open camera menu
  • Take photo or upload a photo of your food
  • Press "Identify" button to identify you food
  • All information about your food will be showed on our app.
  • To show location of every food merchants around you, press "Show Location" button

We hope this short tutorial could guide you to use Foodictive. If you have any other questions or need help, you can reach our developer in the contact section of this web.

Product Featuress

Fast Image Recognition

Our app can recognize food by it's photo in no time. Information about your food will be showed in our app as soon as your photo being uploaded.

Detailed Information

Our app will show every detailed information about your food. Data that provided by our app are recipe, description, and location food seller around you.

Lightweight App

Our app size is small and doesn't need much resources to run it on your phone. So you dont't need to worry about your phone specification to run our app.

Easy to Use
